About the Incident Response Readiness Assessment

Summary

Response to an incident is a critical activity which enables an organization to act rapidly and effectively to address an unexpected cybersecurity disaster. Vancord will perform an Incident Response Readiness Assessment of the core elements that are necessary for a successful recovery from a cyberattack or data breach. This assessment will provide a roadmap that prioritizes improvements necessary to reduce negative outcomes of an incident and emhero a Rapid Response.

The Scope

The scope of this assessment will focus on gathering information related to incident response policies and documentation, business continuity and disaster recovery, log collection and operational practices and evaluation of core network infrastructure. You will have a clear picture of your incident preparedness and your ability to withstand and recover from a cyber attack.

Our assessment will be delivered in a manner consistent with NIST SP 800-62, the NIST Cyber Security Framework, in combination with the extensive experience Vancord brings from previous incident management engagements.
